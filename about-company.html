About Our Company

ISK Construction Pvt Ltd is a trusted provider of sustainable, high-quality engineering solutions in Sri Lanka. Founded by Civil Engineer Indik Boyagoda, our company combines professionalism, quality, and efficiency in every project. Since our start in 2019, we have earned a reputation as a reliable contractor, committed to safety, environmental responsibility, and ISO 9001-certified quality standards. With expertise in water supply, wastewater treatment, industrial construction, and steel fabrication, we proudly serve a range of industries nationwide. Recent projects include essential components for the National Water Supply & Drainage Board (NWSDB) and various industrial facilities.
